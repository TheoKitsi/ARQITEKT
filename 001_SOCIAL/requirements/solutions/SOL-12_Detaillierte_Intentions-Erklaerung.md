# SOL-12: Detailed Intention Declaration

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| User Stories | 1 |
| Components | 1 |
| Functions | 5 |

Even on "serious" platforms there are users with unclear intentions. This module ensures that relationship intention is captured in high detail and weighted in matching. The fields are anchored in the 5-level funnel (SOL-2):

- **Level 1** (mandatory): Relationship intention, time horizon
- **Level 3** (mandatory): Willingness to relocate, maximum distance, long-distance readiness

All intention fields are weighted highly in matching — regardless of their funnel level.

- US-12.1: Detailed Relationship Intention as Mandatory Input
