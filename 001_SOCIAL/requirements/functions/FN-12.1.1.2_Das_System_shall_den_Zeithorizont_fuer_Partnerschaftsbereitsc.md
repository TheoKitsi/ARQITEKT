# FN-12.1.1.2: Partnership Time Horizon

## Specification

The system shall capture the time horizon for partnership readiness as a mandatory field in funnel level 1 (Immediately ready / 1-6 months / 6-12 months / Open). -> Field defined in SOL-2, Level 1.
