# FN-13.1.1.1: Seriousness Score Calculation

## Specification

The system shall calculate a seriousness score from the following factors: verification level (completed/pending), profile completeness (funnel depth), response behavior (response rate and time to matches), activity patterns (regularity of usage), report/block history (number of incoming reports).
