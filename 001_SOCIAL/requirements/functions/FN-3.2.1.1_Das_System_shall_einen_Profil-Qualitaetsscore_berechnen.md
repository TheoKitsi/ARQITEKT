# FN-3.2.1.1: Quality Score Calculation (Matching Impact)

## Specification

The system shall calculate a profile quality score that considers profile completeness (filled mandatory and optional fields), funnel depth (reached levels), and free-text quality. (Same score as SOL-2, FN-2.4.1.2; here the matching impact.)
