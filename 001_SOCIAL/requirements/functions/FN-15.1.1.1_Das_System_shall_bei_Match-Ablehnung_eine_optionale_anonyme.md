# FN-15.1.1.1: Optional Rejection Reasoning

## Specification

The system shall offer an optional, anonymous brief reasoning on match rejection --- with predefined categories (Values, Lifestyle, Distance, Intention, No feeling, Other) and an optional free-text field.
