# FN-16.1.1.5: Strict Level-1 Protection

## Specification

The system shall strictly protect Level-1 fields --- they are never displayed in any view, not even in the SOL-9 compatibility breakdown ('Why This Match?'). Instead, generic paraphrases are used (e.g. 'compatibility in the personal area rated as high').
