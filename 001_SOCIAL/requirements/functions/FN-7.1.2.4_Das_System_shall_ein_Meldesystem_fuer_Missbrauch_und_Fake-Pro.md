# FN-7.1.2.4: Abuse and Fake Profile Reporting

## Specification

The system shall provide a reporting system for abuse and fake profiles; reports are forwarded to a moderation team and processed within a defined SLA. (-> User UI for report function see SOL-5, FN-5.1.2.2)
