# FN-2.1.1.3: Level 3 Self-Description Fields

## Specification

The system shall provide the following self-description fields for Level 3 (Mandatory): desire for children + timeline, career ambitions, income situation, housing goal, willingness to relocate, educational background, family vision, retirement planning attitude.
