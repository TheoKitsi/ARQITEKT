# FN-2.3.1.1: Predefined Particularity Categories

## Specification

The system shall provide predefined particularity categories: lifestyle particularities (e.g., crossdressing, conviction-based veganism, minimalism), sexual preferences (e.g., BDSM, asexuality), unconventional life models (e.g., past open relationship, tiny house living), special family bonds (e.g., close grandparent bond, caregiving responsibility).
