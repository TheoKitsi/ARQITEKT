# FN-12.1.1.1: Relationship Intention Field

## Specification

The system shall provide a mandatory field 'Relationship intention' in funnel level 1 with detailed options (Long-term partnership / Marriage / Living together / Getting to know slowly). -> Field defined in SOL-2, Level 1.
