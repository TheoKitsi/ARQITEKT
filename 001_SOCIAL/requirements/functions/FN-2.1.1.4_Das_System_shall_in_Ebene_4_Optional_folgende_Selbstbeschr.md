# FN-2.1.1.4: Level 4 Self-Description Fields

## Specification

The system shall provide the following self-description fields for Level 4 (Optional): hobbies (detailed), sports/fitness, travel preferences, music taste, sexual preferences/openness, quirks/fetishes, unconventional life models, special family bonds, fears/phobias (partner-relevant).
