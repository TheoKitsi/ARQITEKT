# FN-2.1.1.5: Level 5 Self-Description Fields

## Specification

The system shall provide the following self-description fields for Level 5 (Optional): food preferences (detailed), sleep habits, tidiness/chaos tolerance, social media usage, media consumption, cultural traditions, holidays, humor type, conflict resolution style.
