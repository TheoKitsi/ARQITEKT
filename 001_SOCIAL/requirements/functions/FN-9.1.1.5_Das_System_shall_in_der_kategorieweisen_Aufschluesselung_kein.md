# FN-9.1.1.5: Level-1 Category Suppression

## Specification

The system shall not display categories in the breakdown that contain exclusively level-1 fields --- their compatibility only flows into the overall score without becoming visible as a separate row. This prevents indirect inferences about sensitive level-1 data. (-> SOL-16, FN-16.1.1.5)
