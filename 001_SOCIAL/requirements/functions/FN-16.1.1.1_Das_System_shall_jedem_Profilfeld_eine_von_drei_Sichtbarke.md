# FN-16.1.1.1: Three Visibility Levels Per Field

## Specification

The system shall assign each profile field one of three visibility levels: Level 1 (algorithm only --- data is used for matching but never shown to anyone), Level 2 (match preview --- shown after proposal, before unlock), Level 3 (after unlock --- shown only after mutual consent via SOL-5).
