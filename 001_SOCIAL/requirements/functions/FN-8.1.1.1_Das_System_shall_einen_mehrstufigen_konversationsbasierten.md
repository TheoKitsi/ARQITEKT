# FN-8.1.1.1: Conversational Onboarding Flow

## Specification

The system shall provide a multi-step, conversation-based onboarding assistant that guides the user through all 5 funnel levels --- starting with 'Who am I?' followed by 'Who am I looking for?' per level.
