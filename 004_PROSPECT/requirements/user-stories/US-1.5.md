# US-1.5: Viewing Scheduling

**As a** property owner
**I want to** schedule property viewings with matched candidates
**So that** I can meet and evaluate them before making a lease decision

## Acceptance Criteria

- **AC-1.5.1:** The system shall allow the owner to propose viewing time slots for the property.
- **AC-1.5.2:** The system shall allow matched candidates to select from available time slots or propose alternatives.
- **AC-1.5.3:** The system shall confirm bookings to both parties via email and in-app notification.
- **AC-1.5.4:** The system shall send reminders 24 hours before the viewing.
- **AC-1.5.5:** The system shall allow either party to cancel or reschedule a viewing with at least 12 hours notice.
- **AC-1.5.6:** The system shall track viewing attendance status (attended, cancelled, no-show).
- **AC-1.5.7:** The system shall allow the owner to add viewing notes (private, not shared with candidate).
