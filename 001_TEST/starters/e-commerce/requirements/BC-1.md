# BC-1 — E-Commerce Platform

## WHO
Online retailers who need a digital storefront to sell products.

## WHAT
A full-featured e-commerce platform with product catalog, shopping cart, checkout, and order management.

## WHY
Physical retail alone is insufficient — customers expect 24/7 online shopping with fast search, secure checkout, and order tracking.

## FOR WHOM
Shoppers browsing and purchasing products, and store administrators managing inventory and orders.

## Scope
- Product catalog with search and filtering
- Shopping cart with quantity management
- Secure checkout with payment processing
- Order management and tracking
- Customer accounts and wish lists

## Success Criteria
- Product search returns results within 500ms
- Checkout completes in under 3 steps
- Order confirmation delivered within 60 seconds
