# US-1.2 — Product Detail Page

As a shopper, I want to view product details so that I can make an informed purchase decision.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Page shows product images in a gallery with zoom
2. Variant selection (size, color) updates price and availability
3. Description, specifications, and reviews are shown in tabs
4. Add-to-cart button is prominently displayed with quantity selector
